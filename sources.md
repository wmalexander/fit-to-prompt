## Suggested Daily Workflow

1. **Morning scan** (5 min): The Rundown AI or Superhuman AI newsletter
2. **Company blogs** (5 min): Check Anthropic, OpenAI, Google DeepMind for announcements
3. **Deep reads** (10 min): Simon Willison, Ethan Mollick, or Zvi's latest posts
4. **YouTube** (as published): Fireship, AI Explained, Matt Wolfe for breaking coverage
5. **Weekly**: Hard Fork podcast, Latent Space podcast, Stratechery
